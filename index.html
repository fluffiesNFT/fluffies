Frequently Asked Questions


What are Fluffies?
Fluffies are an experiment in generative pixel art made with the intention of learning, creating a community, and helping animals in need. Stored and created on-chain, Fluffies were built on Ethereum as part of a wave of excitement surrounding digital ownership and identity in the metaverse. We want everyone to be happy with the minting of their own 1/1 Fluffie!
What is saveabunny?
Saveabunny is a California nonprofit established in 1999 with a mission of transforming the lives of rescued rabbits and their prospective owners. To date, SaveABunny has been involved in the adoption of nearly 5,000 rabbits. We are not official associates of SaveABunny, but have long been impressed by them - we're confident each and every Fluffie minted will make a difference in the lives of their real-world cousins. Our hope is to be able to fund them for a full year!
How do I get one?
4,444 Fluffies will be available for minting on October 1st at 9:00pm eastern time. Fluffies can be minted solo for 0.065eth, or in families. We recommend using Metamask for the best experience. If you aren't able to get a Fluffie before they sell out, don't worry! You can likely source one on the secondary market at Opensea.
Who is behind Fluffies?
Fluffies were created by two friends who wanted to combine their respective skills in art and technology to build something they could be proud of on the new frontier of digital property. We wanted to design a collection of art with all the traits needed to allow a true community to grow around Fluffies while avoiding some of the common pitfalls of NFT design like oversupply and lack of originality. The end result was Fluffies: 4,444 procedurally generated friends, each with features carefully animated and a portion of their mint cost going towards a real-world cause that we hope the community can grow a real connection with.
How do I tell if my Fluffie is rare?
As part of our quest to be a different kind of NFT, we've decided to forgo the traditional rarity scores and trait trackers that many NFT projects implement. We think this encourages speculation over enjoyment and stifles the development of a community around Fluffies. We think letting the Fluffies family decide what has value is best!
How were Fluffies made?
After each Fluffie and accessory was carefully imagined and drawn, we spent months building animations to showcase the personalities we wanted Fluffies to have. We built out a process for procedural generation in Python, looking for a balance of true randomness and sure cuteness. Finally using Solidity we launched Fluffies to Ethereum, and now they'll live forever on the blockchain!
